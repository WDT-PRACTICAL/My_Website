We are DayTech, committed to bringing you the best shopping experience with a wide variety of products and unbeatable prices. Our mission is to make online shopping easy and enjoyable.

Meet the Founder & Our Brand

DayTech was founded in 2018 by john Carter, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for simplifying the online shopping experience. With over a decade of experience in the e-commerce industry, John identified a need for a platform that prioritized customer convenience, competitive pricing, and a seamless shopping experience. Her journey began from a small home office, and today, DayTech serves millions of satisfied customers across the globe. John believes in innovation and customer-first solutions, and her leadership has turned DayTech into a trusted name in online retail. She is also a strong advocate for sustainability, ensuring that DayTech partners with environmentally conscious brands.
